My Passion in Life

Always Dancing!

I practice dance since I am 4 years old, it is a sport were I can express myself, be creative and let of steam. Now, I do contemporary dance, jazz and hip hop, and I am also organizing dance class in IESEG Danse association.

Constantly Singing!

I've lived in a musician family, and there always has been music in my house. My mother, father and brother sing all the time, doing harmonies; that's why I started singing with them. The Beatles, Lily Allen, ABBA, Janelle Monae... have marked my musical culture, and I know all their songs by heart.

Travelling

I had the chance to travel a bit with my friends and family. I discovered new landscapes, new cultures, new people. Which built myself! I went to Japan, Iceland, Marocco and even Austria. It was wonderful.
